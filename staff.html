Array in a Box

Create your own custom text box with your word inside!

Here are the steps:

  1. Press play.
  2. Type box("word","symbol") to show the results. (Make sure to include quotation marks!)
  3. Press Enter.
  4. It will create a box with your words inside.

Some tips to look out on:

  • word = any words you want to insert.
  • symbol = any 1 symbol you want to enter.
